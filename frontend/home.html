Why CBC is Different

1

Learning Areas, Not Subjects

We teach integrated learning areas with strands and sub-strands, not isolated subjects.

2

Continuous Assessment

40% School-Based Assessment (SBA) + 60% National Exams (KPSEA at Grade 6).

3

Learner-Centered

Focus on what learners can DO, not just what they know. Practical skills over memorization.

4

Values-Based

Instilling love, responsibility, respect, unity, peace, patriotism, and integrity.

The 7 Core Competencies

Every learner at Rean Primary & Junior Secondary School develops these essential competencies for the 21st century.

💬

Communication & Collaboration

Expressing ideas clearly in English, Kiswahili, and indigenous languages while working effectively with others.

Languages Teamwork
🧠

Critical Thinking & Problem Solving

Analyzing situations logically and finding workable solutions through inquiry-based learning.

Mathematics Science
🎨

Creativity & Imagination

Generating new ideas and thinking beyond conventional limits through arts and innovation.

Arts Design
🇰🇪

Citizenship

Understanding rights, responsibilities, and community values as patriotic Kenyans.

Social Studies Civic Ed
💻

Digital Literacy

Using technology effectively and responsibly for communication and learning.

ICT Coding
📚

Learning to Learn

Developing self-awareness, study habits, and the ability to acquire new knowledge independently.

Research Study Skills
💪

Self-Efficacy

Believing in one's ability to succeed and taking personal initiative in all endeavors.

Leadership Confidence

School-Based Assessment (SBA)

Continuous monitoring through projects, portfolios, practicals, and teacher observations.

60%

National Assessments (KNEC)

KPSEA at Grade 6, Junior School Assessment at Grade 9, and Senior School Assessment at Grade 12.

Parents as Co-Educators

Under CBC, parents play an active role in their child's education through home-based learning and community service activities.

🏠

Home Learning

Supporting practical activities and projects at home that reinforce classroom learning.

📋

Portfolio Support

Helping learners compile evidence of their competencies and achievements.

🌱

Community Service

Participating in community-based learning and service activities with learners.
